### 1. How do I migrate to a new device?
Install the app on the new device and log in. Cloud sync automatically restores settings, preferences, and stored content.

### 2. Why is the microphone not working?
Check **OS microphone permissions**, ensure no other app is locking the microphone, and test using voice notes.

### 3. How do I use offline mode effectively?
Download required resources beforehand. Offline mode caches essential assets and **queues outgoing actions** until reconnection.

### 4. How do I enable developer mode?
Navigate to **Settings → Advanced → Developer Mode**. Features include debug logging, sandbox reset, and test endpoints.

### 5. Why am I getting “operation failed” errors?
These often occur due to expired tokens, incomplete form fields, or network instability. Refreshing the session usually resolves it.

### 6. How do I backup chat history?
Go to **Chat → Settings → Export Chat History**. Choose local backup or cloud archive.

### 7. Why is my storage full even after deleting items?
Deleted items remain in the **Recycle section** until permanently removed. Clear it from **Storage → Recycle**.

### 8. Can I turn off background sync?
Yes. Toggle it off from **Settings → Network → Background Sync**. Manual sync remains available.

### 9. How do I link third-party apps?
Integration settings live under **Settings → Integrations**. Supported services include Drive, Dropbox, and Calendar.

### 10. Why does fingerprint login fail?
Fingerprint sensors struggle with moisture, dirt, or incomplete prints. **Re-registering your fingerprint** usually helps.
